📜 How Challenge Week works

  1. Join once with your Instagram account name and a short password — your points from all five challenges accumulate under it. Changing device? Just Log in with the same name and password.
  2. A new challenge unlocks each day from Monday 27 July to Friday 31 July (and stays open once unlocked).
  3. Play as many times as you like — your best score per day counts.
  4. Come back here anytime to see your total and the leaderboard.
🎁 At the end of the week, show your leaderboard score to the Read4All team (or DM @read4all__) to redeem your mystery gift!

🔒 Privacy: we never see or collect your actual password. It is stored only as an encrypted fingerprint (hash) that cannot be read by anyone — including the Read4All team. Please still avoid reusing an important password.
