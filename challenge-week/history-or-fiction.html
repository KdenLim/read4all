History or Fiction?

Decide whether unusual historical statements are real or made up. Five random flip-cards — build a streak and earn the History Detective badge.

Best score: 0/50
Badge: not earned yet

📜 How to play

  1. Read each strange statement and choose History (it really happened) or Fiction (made up).
  2. The card flips to reveal the truth with a short explanation.
  3. Every correct answer earns 10 points and grows your 🔥 streak.
  4. Answer all 5 in a row correctly to earn the 🏺 History Detective badge.
