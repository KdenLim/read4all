Guess the Place

Read. Explore. Guess.

Six mystery destinations are hiding in these short articles. Read the clues carefully, then take each article's challenge to guess the place and win points.

📜 How to play

  1. Join once on the Challenge Week menu page with your Instagram account name — your points here accumulate under it automatically.
  2. Open a mystery article and read the clues carefully.
  3. Tap "Are You Ready for the Challenge?" and answer the question — every correct answer wins points (a retry costs half).
  4. Solve all six mysteries to complete the game! 🎊
🎁 At the end of the day, show your score on the leaderboard to the Read4All team (or DM @read4all__) to redeem your gift!
