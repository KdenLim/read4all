📜 How to play

  1. Click Start Challenge — 3 random emoji puzzles appear one by one.
  2. Pick the book or story the emojis describe (answers are shuffled every round).
  3. Wrong answer? You get one hint and one retry (worth half points).
  4. Each correct answer earns 10 points and reveals a short story fact.
